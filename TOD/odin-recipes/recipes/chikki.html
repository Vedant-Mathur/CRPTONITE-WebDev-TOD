Coconut Chikki Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 quart grated dried coconut
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup water, or as needed

Directions

  1. Melt 1 tablespoon of ghee in a pan over low heat; add coconut and roast until lightly browned, about 2 minutes. Set aside.
  2. In another skillet over medium heat, mix brown and white sugars. Add ¼ cup water, increase heat, and bring to a boil for about 3 minutes, stirring constantly.
  3. Lower heat and continue stirring until the mixture starts to thicken, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the roasted coconut to the sugar mixture and combine well.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a greased sheet pan or baking dish and spread it evenly.
  6. Cut into squares or rectangles while warm and allow it to cool completely.
  7. Serve and enjoy your coconut chikki!
